03 🔩
PEB Fabrication & Erection

Slab & Structural Strengthening

Engineering-driven slab and structural strengthening solutions for existing buildings requiring load enhancement or damage repair.

05 🔄
Retrofitting & Modification

Structural retrofitting and modification works for buildings requiring seismic upgrades, additions or alterations to existing steel structures.

Mezzanine Steel Floors

Custom and modular mezzanine steel floor structures to maximise usable space in warehouses, factories, and commercial buildings.

PEB Modifications & Alterations

Supply and installation of PUF insulated panels and deck sheets for industrial and commercial roofing and cladding applications.

Founder & Director, 100 Welds

Founded by an Ex-L&T professional with over 15 years of construction execution experience, 100 Welds is a Bengaluru-based structural steel and PEB contractor established in 2014.

We specialise in structural steel fabrication & erection, PEB fabrication & erection, slab & structural strengthening, retrofitting, mezzanine floors, site barricading, and PUF & deck sheet installation.

We serve construction companies, industrial clients, structural engineers, and architects across Bengaluru — delivering engineering-driven solutions with technical clarity and on-time execution.
